Resources

We don’t teach French - we are deliberately a casual group just for conversation, there are no formal lessons and no homework or preparation is required. However here are some helpful links that our members recommend:

Learning

  • Duolingo - a website and smartphone app that teachs French and many other languages gradually, for free (or without ads for an annual fee)
  • Memrise - a paid smartphone app that helps learn vocabulary
  • Rosetta Stone - paid software with some similarities to Duolingo
  • Free French course from TV5MONDE

Dictionaries

  • WordReference - free-to-use (apps also available)
  • Linguee - free-to-use (apps also available)

Immersion: Watching and Listening

  • Radio-Canada podcasts - practice listening or just immerse yourself in the sounds
  • 239 French Conversations on YouTube
  • Language Learning with Netflix - an extension for the Chrome browser - if you have a Netflix subscription, it can show subtitles in your native language and another at the same time

News in French

Canada

  • ICI Radio-Canada - Canada’s public broadcasting
  • La Presse - independent, non-profit Québec news organisation

World

  • Courrier International - French language news from a variety of sources
  • africanews - Pan-African news network
  • Euronews - Pan-European news network
  • France 24 - 24-hour international news TV station with a live feed, owned by the French Government (also available on YouTube)
  • Radio France Internationale - French world public radio service

Online Conversation Groups

  • This group on Meetup
  • Other French groups on Meetup - within 100km of London
  • Duolingo Events has a lot of meetings, including in French

Local Information and Learning

  • Vivre en Français à London - list of French resources from the City of London, Ontario
  • L’Action - London, Ontario area news in French
  • Collège Boréal - offers French-as-a-Second-Language courses at their downtown campus in Citi Plaza
  • Western Continuing Studies - offers French classes at their downtown Citi Plaza location
  • Thames Valley District School Board - offer Core French and French Immersion programs for schoolkids
  • The London, Ontario article on French-language Wikipedia
